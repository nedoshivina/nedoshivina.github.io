Glossary

Word Interpretation Translation
1)Burner The part of a cooker, etc. That produces a flame Горелка
2)Chemicals A substance obtained by or used in a chemical process Химикаты
3)Cryogenic procedures Procedures using very low temperatures Криогенные процедуры
4)Dispersion (of aerosols) The process by which people or things are spread over a wide area Рассеивание (аэрозоля)
5)Dosage It is batch or continuous weighing or volumetric metering of raw materials in the quantities provided by the recipes Дозирование
6)Ergonomic injury Injuries resulting from improper performance of general movements Эргономическая травма
7)Flammable That can burn easily Легковоспламеняющийся
8)Fume hood Laboratory equipment, which is a closed chamber with exhaust ventilation for working with volatile hazardous substances Вытяжной шкаф
9)Grinding A process using an abrasive tool, the cutting elements of which are particles of abrasive materials. Шлифование
10)Perforated Perforate something to make a hole or holes through something Перфорированный
11)Pipetting It is batch or continuous weighing or volumetric metering of raw materials in the quantities provided by the recipes Пипетирование
12)Pressure The force acting on some n. Surface; degree of elasticity of gases and liquids Давление
13)Pyrophoric It is the ability of substances to ignite spontaneously on contact with air. Пирофорность
14)Reagents A substance used to cause a chemical reaction, especially in order to find out if another substance is present Реагенты
15)Safety precautions A system of organizational measures, technical means and methods to prevent exposure to hazardous production factors Меры безопасности
16)Siphon A tube that is used to move liquid from one container down into another, lower container Сифон
17)Soldering To join pieces of metal or wire with solder Пайка
18)Toxicity The fact of being poisonous; the extent to which something is poisonous Токсичность
19)Waste Substances or objects that are formed in the process of production, performance of work, provision of services or in the process of consumption, which are processed, disposed of or disposed of Отходы
